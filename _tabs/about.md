

# About Me

I am Wenze Xiong, a PhD student in Finance at the University of Auckland.

My research interests include financial technology, cryptocurrency, digital currency, blockchain, digital economy, decentralized finance, and machine learning applications in financial markets.

I am particularly interested in digital assets, alternative assets, data valuation, and the interaction between financial markets and emerging technologies.

## Research Interests

* Financial technology
* Cryptocurrency and digital currency
* Blockchain and decentralized finance
* Digital economy
* Alternative assets and NFTs
* Machine learning for financial prediction
* Textual data valuation and data markets
